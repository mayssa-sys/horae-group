What We Do

Comprehensive Business Solutions

Human expertise at the core, enhanced by technology. We provide end-to-end consulting across sales, marketing, real estate, and events — tailored for MENA and Africa.

📊

Sales Management Consulting

We roll up our sleeves and work alongside your team to deliver real results. Starting with a thorough sales team assessment, we identify performance gaps, process inefficiencies, and missed opportunities — then restructure your entire operation for growth.

We redesign your sales process, set actionable KPIs, implement CRM systems, and build accountability frameworks. We also specialize in customer experience and journey optimization — mapping every touchpoint to uncover where prospects drop off and convert more effectively. Our consultants bring field experience across retail, FMCG, real estate, fintech, and agriculture, combining practical execution with AI-powered analytics.

🎓

Training & Coaching

No one-size-fits-all here. We start with a comprehensive assessment of your team — identifying skills gaps, understanding your culture, and diagnosing what's really holding performance back. Then we design a customized solution that fits your people.

Our programs blend hands-on exercises with essential skills: communication, negotiation, objection handling, CRM proficiency, and AI tool adoption. We favor realistic scenarios over theory — roleplay, live coaching, field shadowing, and post-training follow-ups. Whether it's a focused workshop or multi-week executive coaching, every session is built for lasting, measurable improvement.

🎯

Digital Marketing

We're not here to make things look pretty — we're here to increase your sales. Every strategy, campaign, and piece of content is designed with a sales-first methodology aimed at driving revenue.

We build your entire digital presence in the direction of your sales funnel — attracting the right prospects, nurturing them, and converting them into paying customers. Covering SEO, social media, content creation, paid advertising, and email marketing, we continuously optimize with AI-enhanced analytics to maximize your ROI across every channel.

🏗️

Real Estate Services

The MENA real estate market demands deep local knowledge and strong relationships. We provide end-to-end consulting — from market analysis and feasibility studies to sales strategy, team training, and project launch execution.

Whether you're a developer launching a project, a brokerage scaling operations, or an investor entering a new market, we position your property, reach the right buyers, and close deals efficiently. Our digital tools including virtual tours and AI-powered lead matching complement the human touch that seals the deal.

🎪

Event Management

From corporate conferences to product launches and networking events, we craft memorable experiences that drive real business outcomes. Our team handles everything — concept to execution.

We manage venue sourcing, vendor coordination, branding, logistics, and on-the-day execution. Events create human connections no digital channel can replace. We also leverage digital tools for registration, attendee engagement, live analytics, and post-event follow-up to ensure measurable impact beyond the day itself.

🤖

AI-Powered Sales Optimization

For businesses ready to take the next step, we layer AI and automation on top of solid sales foundations. Predictive lead scoring, intelligent pipeline forecasting, and real-time performance dashboards.

We implement automated follow-up sequences and give your managers instant visibility into performance. We don't replace people with technology — we give your people superpowers. Every AI implementation is designed to free your team from repetitive tasks so they can focus on building relationships and closing deals.

About Us

Born in Crisis,
Built to Prevail

Horae Group was founded in 2019 in Beirut, Lebanon — just weeks before the country plunged into its worst financial crisis in modern history. When the world around us was collapsing, many said building a business was impossible. But we refused to accept that.

Against all odds, we didn't just survive — we thrived. With unwavering determination and the relentless effort of our team, we grew from a small Beirut office into a regional force. We founded Horae Egypt in partnership with GICG Group, and expanded our reach into the GCC and Africa. Every setback became fuel. Every challenge became a lesson. And every person who believed in us became part of our story.

Our philosophy remains simple: it's about people. People buy from people. Teams succeed because of people. And technology should empower people — not replace them. That's why we blend hands-on human expertise with AI-enhanced tools to deliver solutions that are practical, culturally grounded, and results-driven.
